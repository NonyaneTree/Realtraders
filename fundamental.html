üìò Understanding Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis is a method used by traders to evaluate currencies by studying economic data, central bank decisions, and global events that influence supply and demand.

  • Interest Rates: Higher rates usually strengthen a currency.
  • Inflation (CPI & PPI): Rising inflation may weaken purchasing power.
  • GDP: Measures economic growth and stability.
  • Employment Data: NFP and unemployment rate strongly impact volatility.
  • Central Bank Statements: Forward guidance often moves markets before price reacts.
