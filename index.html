What is Forex Trading?

Forex (Foreign Exchange) trading is the process of buying and selling currencies to profit from price movements. It is the largest financial market in the world, operating 24 hours a day, five days a week.

Traders analyze currency pairs such as EUR/USD, GBP/USD, and USD/JPY. If you expect the price to rise, you buy. If you expect it to fall, you sell.

How Forex Works

  • Currencies are traded in pairs
  • Prices move based on supply and demand
  • Economic news and interest rates influence the market
  • Risk management is essential for consistency
