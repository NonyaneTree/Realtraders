About the Owner

Thulane Nonyane — Founder & CEO of Nonyane Investment Tree

Thulane Nonyane is the visionary founder and CEO of Nonyane Investment Tree, a forward-looking investment company focused on empowering Africa's future through strategic investments in undervalued businesses. With a deep understanding of the stock market, cryptocurrency, and ETF creation, he aims to bridge the gap between traditional finance and modern digital opportunities.

Under his leadership, Nonyane Investment Tree is not just a company—it's a mission to become South Africa’s most influential investment brand. The company’s signature project is the NonyaneInvest Token, an asset-backed digital token tied to shares in real businesses, designed to give the low and middle class a safer gateway into investing.

Thulane’s investment strategy includes acquiring stakes in companies like African Dawn Capital, Visual International Holdings, and RMB Holdings, with a vision to grow their value and tokenize them into ETFs available for independent investors. This model is built on sustainability, growth, and accessibility for all.

As a leader, Thulane is known for his discipline, long-term thinking, and passion for financial empowerment. He also understands the need for steady income, planning the company’s cash flow through stable crypto staking like USTC and employing a lean, skilled team to execute the company vision efficiently.

In the near future, Thulane aims to expand operations across the African continent, providing opportunities, creating jobs, and building a financial legacy that generations can rely on.

"The goal is not just wealth, but impact." – Thulane Nonyane
